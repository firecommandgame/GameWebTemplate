About TravelVIP

TravelVIP was created to simplify group travel.

Planning trips with friends and family often involves scattered messages, spreadsheets, and confusion over flights and expenses.

TravelVIP brings everything together into one collaborative platform where travelers can organize itineraries, manage shared expenses, coordinate flights, and stay synchronized in real time.

Our mission is simple:

Travel better. Together.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to TravelVIP.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how TravelVIP collects, uses, stores, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we may collect:

Name
Email address
Home airport (if provided)
Trip information
Travel plans
Flight information
Shared expenses
Group membership information

Authentication

TravelVIP uses Firebase Authentication to securely manage user accounts.

Passwords are never stored directly by TravelVIP.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Create and manage your account
Synchronize trips across devices
Allow invited members to collaborate
Store travel information
Improve app performance

Information Sharing

TravelVIP does not sell your personal information.

Trip information is shared only with users you invite to your trips.

Data Security

TravelVIP uses Google Firebase services with industry-standard encryption and security practices.

Data Retention

Your information remains stored until you delete your account or request deletion.

Children's Privacy

TravelVIP is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
