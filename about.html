About Hotel Games

Building the future of simulation and serious games through innovation, creativity, and player-focused development.

Our Story

Hotel Games Studios is a developer and marketer of games worldwide. With a focus on simulation and serious games, our studio intends on fulfilling a market hungry for interactive gameplay neglected by major gaming developers.

Our Philosophy

Hotel Games seeks to cement itself among gaming studios by treating our employees with dignity and respect, focusing on innovation, and building customer loyalty by introducing game features that leave a lasting impression and listening to our consumer base as to what they would like to see in our games. At Hotel Games Studios, software development is not just a profit-making venture but a way of life. Hotel Games Studios will empower our modelers, animators, and programmers to develop games they would like to play. Hotel Games will revolutionize gaming in a way that has not been previously seen before.
