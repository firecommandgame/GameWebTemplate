Fire Command

Fire Command is a serious game. Players command fire rescue units and respond to fire, medical, hazardous materials, technical rescue, and specialty emergencies while managing resources across a dynamic city map. Featuring realistic dispatch communications, incident command, patient assessment and treatment, fire attack strategies, search and rescue operations, apparatus management, weather and traffic effects, and dynamic call generation, Fire Command blends the experience of a video game with a high-fidelity emergency services simulation to create a dynamic teaching, leadership, and tactical decision-making tool.

Police Command

Police Command is a serious game. Players command law enforcement units, respond to emergency calls, conduct traffic stops and investigations, execute warrants, manage critical incidents, and allocate resources across a dynamic city map. Featuring customizable scenarios, crime statistics, BOLO and missing person alerts, interrogation techniques, surveillance operations, tactical planning tools, and realistic officer safety mechanics, Police Command blends the experience of a video game with an immersive police simulation to create a dynamic teaching and tactical decision-making tool.

Fire Command for iOS

Fire Command for iOS brings emergency response simulation to mobile devices with streamlined controls, incident management, dispatch operations, and tactical decision-making designed for quick sessions anywhere.
